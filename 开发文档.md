## 3. 架构说明
当前采用：
- `IntentRouterAgent`：意图识别（LLM 结构化输出 + 置信度阈值）
- `SearchAgent`：查询类
- `RAGAgent`：规则类
- `OrderAgent`：订单类（LangChain chain 强控制）
- `SummarizerAgent`：最终回复整合
- `MultiAgentOrchestrator`：基于 LangGraph 的图编排

编排图（当前实现）：
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    resolveRoute[resolveRoute]
    queryNode[queryAgent]
    ruleNode[ruleAgent]
    orderNode[orderAgent]
    unknownNode[unknownAgent]
    summarizeNode[summarize]
    endNode[END]

    resolveRoute -->|query| queryNode
    resolveRoute -->|rule| ruleNode
    resolveRoute -->|order| orderNode
    resolveRoute -->|unknown| unknownNode
    queryNode --> summarizeNode
    ruleNode --> summarizeNode
    orderNode --> summarizeNode
    unknownNode --> summarizeNode
    summarizeNode --> endNode
```
